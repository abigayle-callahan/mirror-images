OUR STORY

About Leisha

Leisha’s passion for hair began at a young age.  As a young girl, she could be found styling the hair of her barbie dolls and that soon progressed to the hair of babysitters and neighbors.

After High school, Leisha followed her passion to pursue her cosmetology license at Empire Beauty School, and has now owned and operated her own salon for 33 years.

Leisha believes “knowledge is power” and continues to educate herself on the latest hair trends.  She currently works behind the chair at her own salon in Wolfeboro, NH.  Leisha is great with hair color and loves changing up her guest’s image.
